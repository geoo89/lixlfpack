Lemmings Forums Pack

This level pack for Lix is a
joint work by forum users on
www.lemmingsforums.com.

240 levels await you, arranged in
six ratings of 40 levels each
from Simple (easy to medium)
through Hopeless (very hard).

Proliferous level authors are:

Insane Steve, Michael,
geoo, mobius, Nortaneous.

Additional authors have made
some more levels in this pack.

Have fun!
